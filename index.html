The Genius of The Good Place

A show analysis by Corinne Llantero

Description

The Good Place, created by Michael Schur, is that fantasy afterlife comedy series. It revolves around Eleanor Shellstrop, who is mistakenly sent to the Heaven-like paradise in the show. As she goes through her journey to hide this error, she meets new people who help her become a better person and discovers the secrets behind this seemingly perfect utopia. This website will analyze and interpret the technical features of the show and the intricate symbolism behind its writing. It will focus on three aspects: the use of comedy, ethics and philosophy, and storytelling. Just a warning: this will contain spoilers for the entire show!
